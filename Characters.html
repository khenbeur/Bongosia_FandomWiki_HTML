Zani

Zani is a 5-star Spectro Mutant Resonator in Wuthering Waves, wielding gauntlets and serving as a security staff member at the Montelli Family's Averardo Bank in Ragunna.

Key Attributes:

Spectro, a damage type that enables her to apply and benefit from the Spectro Frazzle debuff.

Lupa

Lupa is a 5-star Fusion Non-congenital Resonator in Wuthering Waves, wielding a broadblade and hailing from Septimont in Rinascita.

Key Attributes:

5-star, DPS, Fusion Non-congenital Resonator

Mornye

Playable Fusion Natural Resonator in Wuthering Waves. She is a researcher for the Spacetrek Collective and a professor for Exostrider Engineering at Startorch Academy. She was a student at Startorch 20 years ago before the Void Storm hit Lahai-Roi.

Key Attributes:

Fusion, enabling her to power up Fusion-based teams.

Lynae

Playable Spectro Congenital Resonator in Wuthering Waves. She is a Preparatory Program student at Startorch Academy. Before attending Startorch she was a mercenary from the Lawless Zone in New Federation. In order to escape from the Lawless Zone she stole the identity of the real Lynae—a Fractsidus operative.

Key Attributes:

Spectro, enabling synergy with Spectro-based teams and debuffs like Photochromic Flux.

Augusta

Playable Electro Congenital Resonator in Wuthering Waves. The fearless Ephor of Septimont with a will of iron, she remains a fiercely resilient yet humble leader despite hardships and her past as a Gladiator.

Key Attributes:

Electro, enabling synergy with Electro-based teams and heavy attacks.

Iuno

Playable Aero Congenital Resonator in Wuthering Waves. A renowned Priestess of Septimont's Tetragon Temple who delivers prophecies while defying fate.

Key Attributes:

Aero, enabling synergy with Aero-based teams and Heavy attacks DPS.
